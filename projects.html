Timber treatment autoclave

This Machine made in several procedures such as Designing, Cutting, welding, asembling and automation. In this proccess, at the beginning, vacuum pump pulls out air and moisture from the timbers for few hours. Then water and chemicals pumps into the tank under 12-13 bars of pressure and remains for 10-12 hours. There is another vacuuming proccess after draining the chemicals to decrease the moisture of treated wood as much as possible.

Arooz angled-seat valve

Pnuematic valve is device that is used to control the flow of fluid in any system. Angled-seat is kind of pneumatically controlled valve with a piston actuator providing linear actuation to lift a seal off its seat. The seat is set at an angle to provide the maximum possible flow when unseated.
Due to lack of product of this specific type of pnuematic valve in our country, despite various use of it in different industries, I and friend of mine, invested on this start-up. High-quality and long lasting product is our company's main policy.
We modeled our own body stracture and made aluminium mold out of it. Each valve contains 4 different casted parts and a head formed by hydraulic pressing. Casting and CNC-machining proccesses are being outsourced. Each valve contains about 30 parts and it's being assembled in our warehouse. At the moment, we produce 6 different sizes from DN15 to DN50.
