Job career

2007

When I was studying textile engineering in Isfahan University of Technology, I've been working for Diba control as a trainee for part time. Then I started to learn about industrial electricity and automation systems. In June 2008 I started an internship in Alvan Naghsh Pood fabric dyeing factory. I worked there during summer holidays and also weekends during other study seasons. I Learned a lot practical skills there and got to know about how industrial machines work.

2009

I started to co-operate with Mehrsan machinary as a PLC programmer and designer to set up first textile manufacturing company in Iran. We designed and produced fabric dryer machine, Ballon squeezer machine, Rop opening machin and Fabric finish compactor machines for the first time in Iran. We installed our machines in Tehran, Isfahan, Yazd, Mashhad and also Peru.

2014

As soon as I graduated from IUT in Jan 2014, I employed as a rapair and maintenance manager in Alvan Naghsh Pood company. I managed to produce a High-Temprature fabric dyeing machine for the first time in Iran. I spent almost 2 years of my career on that project due to lack of technical and financial support. I managed to automate and renew the whole production line as well.

2017

I decided to serve my military service. So I quit my job out of nessecity. But I was still working with both Alvan naghsh pood and Mehrsan companies as an automation engineer for the projects that didn't need to spend a lot of time continiuesly. I also improved some skills such as HTML/CSS, Python and ... to prevent my time being wasted during that period. I code my own website in boostrap framework.

2019

After I finished my millitary service, I started up High-temprature fabric dyeing machinary company. I produced 3 machines from 2019 until may 2021. It was fully designed by me. I tried to produce most parts of the machine. Even dosages pumps and electricity panel and valves and tanks were made in my warehouse from 0 to 100.

2019

I also started up the Arooz pnuematic angled-seat valve company with a friend of mine to support my machinery and avoid importing valves and reduce the cost. We produce valves in different sizes from DN15 up to DN50. This specific type of valve also was made in Iran for the first time.
